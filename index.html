Ludo Game Rules

  • 🎲 Start: Roll a 6 to move a token out of your starting yard.
  • ➕ Extra Roll: Rolling a 6 or capturing a token grants an extra turn.
  • 💥 Capture: Landing on an opponent's token sends it back home!
  • ⭐ Safe Zones: Star-marked tiles protect tokens from being captured.
  • 🏆 Victory: Get all 4 tokens to the home triangle to win!

How to Play Ludo Web Games

Ludo is a strategy board game for two to four players, in which the players race their four tokens from start to finish according to the rolls of a single die. The basic loop is simple: roll the dice, select one of your highlighted tokens to move, capture opponent pieces when landing on the same coordinate, and reach the home triangle first.
